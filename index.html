DeciSpin

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

🔒 DeciSpin does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not have accounts, logins, or servers. All your data stays on your device.

1. Overview

DeciSpin ("the app") is a decision-making tool that helps you choose between options using a spinning wheel. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

By using DeciSpin, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

DeciSpin does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

  • Wheels you create and save
  • AI suggestion history
  • App settings and preferences

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings.

4. Third-Party APIs

DeciSpin uses the following third-party services to fetch content (movie titles, music tracks, game names, etc.). When you browse a category, the app sends a request to these services. Please refer to their own privacy policies for details on how they handle requests.

TheTVDB thetvdb.com

Used for TV series and movie data. Privacy Policy

Last.fm last.fm

Used for music track and genre data. Privacy Policy

Apple iTunes Search API apple.com

Used for music artwork images. Privacy Policy

TMDB (The Movie Database) themoviedb.org

Used for movie data and images. Privacy Policy

FreeToGame freetogame.com

Used for free-to-play game data. Privacy Policy

TheMealDB themealdb.com

Used for food and cuisine data. themealdb.com

Anthropic Claude API anthropic.com

Used to power the AI suggestion feature. When you use the AI tab, your prompt is sent to Anthropic's servers. Privacy Policy

5. AI Feature

When you use the AI suggestion feature, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate recommendations. This text is processed by Anthropic in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store or log the prompts you send.

We recommend not entering sensitive personal information in the AI prompt.

6. Children's Privacy

DeciSpin does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not contain features that would require personal data from any user, regardless of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
